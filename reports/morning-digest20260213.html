Section 3: Sector Deep Dives

Theme of the Week: "From Narrative to Proof"

The honeymoon phase of AI is ending. Markets are no longer rewarding "ambition" or "announced capex." Instead, the filter has tightened toward Physical Infrastructure and Monetizable Scarcity. The bottleneck has moved from software code to power availability, fab throughput (TSMC), and regulatory/zoning approvals. We are seeing a divergence where "Interface Software" is being sold off as a replacement risk, while "Systems of Record" and "Infrastructure Backbone" (Energy, Data Centers) remain high-conviction holds.
