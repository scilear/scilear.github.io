Theme of the Week: Microcaps & AI

The core thesis from our sources suggests a rotation away from traditional "Big Tech" (the original Magnificent 7) into "Little Tech" or Microcaps. The argument is that while giants like Nvidia aren't finished, the exponential "bang for your buck" is shifting to smaller, undervalued players that are indispensable partners to the giants (e.g., providing data to Palantir, chips to Nvidia, or AI integration for Autos).

Key Drivers:

  • AI Supply Chain: Moving beyond the chip makers to the data providers (INOD) and implementers (CRNC).
  • Asset-Heavy Tech: Robotics (RR) and specialized manufacturing (NNDM) are gaining attention over pure software plays.
  • Crypto/DeFi: Despite recent volatility, infrastructure plays (DEFT) are being accumulated.
